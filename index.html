Key Findings
What the analysis surfaced. Every number below is computed live from the 500 SKU dataset.
1. $2.67M of excess capital sits in 85 SKUs: slow movers, stock aged past 90 days, and $216K locked in EOL products awaiting disposition.
2. 30 SKUs are at or below reorder point right now, including high value A items inside their lead time window. Immediate purchase orders required.
3. Forecast MAPE on forecastable items is 14.0%. Stable X items run under 10%. Erratic Z items were moved to a Min Max policy instead of forecasting.
4. Two suppliers (Pacific Semis and Busan Display Tech) carry roughly 43% of annual spend while flagging HIGH RISK on delivery and lead time. A concentration problem.
5. Q4 demand runs nearly double the baseline, so seasonal SKUs need seasonal methods, not moving averages. Try the Replenishment Lab to stress test the policy levers.
